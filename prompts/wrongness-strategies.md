# Wrongness Strategies

A compact taxonomy of the moves the system makes. Each strategy names a specific pattern of feed-native misreading. One-sentence definitions — the full unpacking is in the artistic brief.

- **listicle_flattening** — a modal statement is rendered as a numbered, scannable list that strips its moral grain.
- **spotify_mood_tag** — a communal mode is reduced to a private playlist label under a genre/mood taxonomy.
- **aesthetic_commerce** — a cultural palette is extracted from its musical grammar and resold as a fashion drop or decor theme.
- **wellness_pacification** — grief that is meant to be ritualized together is converted into a solitary self-soothing exercise.
- **reward_loop** — a celebratory phrase is rewritten as a dopamine-schedule reward prompt with a streak counter.

Session escalation rule: once a strategy appears in `session_memory.wrongness_strategies`, subsequent phrases may repeat it or move to a louder one, but never to a softer one. The monotonic non-decrease of `escalation_level` is enforced by the state machine, not the schema.
